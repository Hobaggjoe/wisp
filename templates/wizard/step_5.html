Vendor Risk

Third-party vendors are a common source of data breaches. Proper vendor management is essential.

  • • 60% of breaches involve third parties
  • • Due diligence is legally required
  • • Contracts must specify security requirements

Common Vendors to Include

  • • IT support companies
  • • Cloud service providers
  • • Software vendors
  • • Cleaning services (if they access offices)
  • • Consultants and contractors
  • • Legal and accounting firms
  • • Backup and recovery services

Key Contract Elements

  • • Data encryption requirements
  • • Access controls and authentication
  • • Incident notification timelines
  • • Audit rights and compliance reporting
  • • Data deletion upon contract termination
