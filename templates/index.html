Frequently Asked Questions

What is a WISP?

A Written Information Security Plan (WISP) is a document that outlines how your business protects sensitive customer information, required by IRS regulations and GLBA.

Who needs a WISP?

Any business that handles sensitive customer information, including accounting firms, financial advisors, legal practices, and healthcare providers.

How long does it take?

Most businesses can complete the wizard and generate their WISP in 15-30 minutes, depending on the complexity of their operations.

Is the generated WISP legally compliant?

Yes, our templates are based on IRS Publication 4557 and GLBA requirements. However, we recommend reviewing with legal counsel for your specific situation.
